# MindPrism Brand Guidelines V2 (Dark Purple)

## Brand Personality
- Smart
- Calm
- Premium
- Focused

## Token System
- `--bg-950: #0A0612`
- `--bg-900: #120A1F`
- `--surface-800: #1D1030`
- `--surface-700: #291746`
- `--primary-500: #7C3AED`
- `--primary-400: #8B5CF6`
- `--accent-400: #C4B5FD`
- `--text-100: #F5F3FF`
- `--text-300: #DDD6FE`

## Typography
- Display: `Sora`
- Body/UI: `IBM Plex Sans`
- Utility labels: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Logo Rules
1. Use official icon asset only.
2. Keep safe area around icon equal to 0.5x icon height.
3. Wordmark color on dark surfaces: `#F5F3FF`.
4. Never place logo on low-contrast noisy backgrounds.

## Button Rules
- Primary button reserved for highest-priority action.
- Avoid multiple primary buttons adjacent in same section.
- Keep consistent radius, height, and motion timings.
- Disabled states must remain readable with clear affordance.

## Surface Rules
- Use layered panel contrast instead of heavy gradients.
- Keep shadows soft and minimal.
- Avoid neon glow overuse; use accent glows sparingly.

## Layout Rules
- Mobile-first.
- CTA within thumb reach on primary tasks.
- Keep vertical rhythm consistent (8pt grid).

## Accessibility
- AA contrast baseline.
- Visible focus ring.
- Reduced motion support.
