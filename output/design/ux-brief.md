# UX Brief V2: MindPrism Dark Purple Redesign

## Goal
Upgrade MindPrism into a more premium, modern, and slick product experience while preserving clarity and usability.

## Design Intent
- Keep the app calm and content-first.
- Shift brand expression to a dark-purple identity.
- Improve logo/wordmark presence and visual consistency.
- Upgrade button quality, hierarchy, and interaction feedback.

## Visual Direction
- Atmosphere: cinematic, confident, focused.
- Surface style: layered dark panels with subtle glow edges.
- Information style: high contrast, clear spacing, minimal clutter.

## Typography
- Display: `Sora`
- Body/UI: `IBM Plex Sans`
- Meta/labels: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Logo + Wordmark Guidance
1. Keep logo icon visible in header with clear breathing room.
2. Pair icon with `MindPrism` wordmark in `Sora` semibold.
3. Avoid tiny low-contrast wordmark placements on dark surfaces.
4. Use one locked layout:
- Compact: icon + wordmark horizontal
- Hero: icon above wordmark with controlled spacing

## UX Priorities
1. Onboarding feels premium and simple in 3 steps.
2. Dashboard shows immediate value (resume, recommendations, progress).
3. Buttons communicate hierarchy instantly.

## Competitor Inspiration (Pattern-only)
- Linear: fast interaction and clean hierarchy.
- Notion: clean content surfaces.
- Asana: progress visibility.
- ClickUp: modular card organization.
